Portfolio Risk is a vital, but often overlooked, aspect of investments. The main reason is that it is harder to track over time. For many investors, the information given to them isn't specific enough to their portfolio, meaning it's harder to see how risky their portfolio is. This makes tracking the risk of a portfolio, or spotting outliers in a specific portfolio, harder and more tedious. This project aims to provide a streamlined platform for users to see the past risk of their portfolios, find stocks affecting their overall returns, or compare their portfolio to other top-performing stocks. 

My senior project is a dashboard that loads in a portfolio and then visualizes the risk metrics over time. The different metrics will be graphed in a time series, allowing users to identify outliers in their portfolios and draw conclusions from the visualizations over time. The plan is to plot each stock's running alpha and beta, based on a 3-month time horizon, dating back to 2 years.
